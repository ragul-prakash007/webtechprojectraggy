About FWRCFN Tamil Nadu

Every day, tons of edible food is wasted across Tamil Nadu while many people struggle for meals. Our platform connects restaurants, hotels, marriage halls, and individuals with volunteers and community fridges to create a sustainable solution to food waste and hunger.
